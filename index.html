our philosophy

The assumption that most people do not care about User Agreements or Privacy Policies is both inaccurate and incomplete. Usually terms are lengthy and full of jargon, and are delivered in a way that hinders reading and understanding the details. Until we came along, no one had made the effort to present the legal policies in a simplified, convenient & understandable manner.
