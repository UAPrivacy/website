Our Philosophy

The assumption that people do not care about Terms of Service or Privacy Policy is both inaccurate and incomplete. Usually these agreements are long-winded and poorly formatted thus hindering most people from reading them. We strive to present the summaries of important clauses in a simpler but convenient format.
